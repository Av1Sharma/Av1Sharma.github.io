Project Details

SpotiStats is an app that helps users track and analyze their Spotify listening habits, providing insights such as most listened songs, genres, and time spent on music.

  • Real-time user statistics analysis including:
    • Most popular listening hours
    • Top artists and tracks
    • Customizable time intervals (4 weeks, 6 months, all-time)
  • Data visualization and analysis
  • Customizable insights based on listening patterns

Built with: Python, pandas, yfinance, etc.
